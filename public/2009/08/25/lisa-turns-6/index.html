Lisa turns 6!

2009-08-25 — Written by Lisa
#birthday  #family  #friends  #fun  #sixth birthday 

Today, Lisa turned six years old!  She got to take treats to school (which is a Dutch tradition called “trakteren”).  We also had a birthday party for her at Heidebeek for everyone.  It was great fun!

Her Aunt Sylvia and her cousins Sabine and Valerie were also here for a couple of days.  Uncle Jan and Aunt Cindy and Opa were visiting in the weekend.

Happy Birthday, Lisa!

Who made Jesus?

2009-04-07 — Written by Lisa
#eternity  #God  #Jesus  #tough questions 

While putting my daughter to bed the other night, she asked me a whopper of a question.

If Jesus made us, then who made Jesus? Well, how do you explain that to a five-year-old?

My best answer was that He has always been there.

Her response: “Well, if He has always been there, then 100 is not very old.”

“Yes, you’re right, 100 is not very old,” I said.
